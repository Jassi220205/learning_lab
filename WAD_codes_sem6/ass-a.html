Glamour Gala 2026

About the Event

Glamour Gala 2026 is a premier inter-college fashion showcase organized to encourage innovation, creativity, and self-expression among aspiring designers.

Event Highlights

Runway walks, sustainable design showcases, makeup masterclasses, and styling workshops conducted by renowned fashion designers and industry experts.

Eligibility

Open to all students with a passion for fashion and design. Participants can register individually or in design teams.

Venue Details

Grand Ballroom, The Royal Plaza, Visakhapatnam.
